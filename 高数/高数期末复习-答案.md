### 答案 1 — 填空题（二元函数极限）

$$
\lim_{(x,y) \to (0,0)} \frac{\sin(xy)}{x} = \lim_{(x,y) \to (0,0)} \frac{xy}{x} = \lim_{(x,y) \to (0,0)} y = \boxed{0}
$$

> 思路：$\sin(xy) \sim xy$（等价无穷小），约去 $x$ 后直接代入

### 答案 2 — 计算题（一阶偏导数）

$z = e^{xy}\sin(x+y)$

**对 $x$ 求偏导**（$y$ 视为常数）：

$$
\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} = ye^{xy}\sin(x+y) + e^{xy}\cos(x+y) = \boxed{e^{xy}[y\sin(x+y) + \cos(x+y)]}
$$

**对 $y$ 求偏导**（$x$ 视为常数）：

$$
\frac{\partial z}{\partial y} = xe^{xy}\sin(x+y) + e^{xy}\cos(x+y) = \boxed{e^{xy}[x\sin(x+y) + \cos(x+y)]}
$$

### 答案 3 — 计算题（二阶偏导数）

$z = f(u,v)$，$u = x^2 - y^2$，$v = e^{xy}$

**第一步：求一阶偏导**

$$
\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} = f_1 \cdot 2x + f_2 \cdot ye^{xy}
$$

**第二步：对 $y$ 求偏导（注意 $f_1$、$f_2$ 仍是 $u,v$ 的函数）**

$$
\frac{\partial^2 z}{\partial y \partial x} = \frac{\partial}{\partial y}(f_1 \cdot 2x) + \frac{\partial}{\partial y}(f_2 \cdot ye^{xy})
$$

**展开第一项**：

$$
\frac{\partial}{\partial y}(f_1 \cdot 2x) = 2x \cdot \frac{\partial f_1}{\partial y} = 2x(f_{11} \cdot (-2y) + f_{12} \cdot xe^{xy})
$$

**展开第二项**：

$$
\frac{\partial}{\partial y}(f_2 \cdot ye^{xy}) = \frac{\partial f_2}{\partial y} \cdot ye^{xy} + f_2 \cdot \frac{\partial(ye^{xy})}{\partial y}
$$

$$
= (f_{21} \cdot (-2y) + f_{22} \cdot xe^{xy}) \cdot ye^{xy} + f_2 \cdot (e^{xy} + xye^{xy})
$$

**合并**（利用 $f_{12} = f_{21}$）：

$$
\boxed{\frac{\partial^2 z}{\partial y \partial x} = -4xyf_{11} + 2x^2e^{xy}f_{12} - 2y^2e^{xy}f_{12} + xye^{2xy}f_{22} + 2xf_1e^{xy} + f_2e^{xy}(1+xy)}
$$

> ⚠️ 关键：$f_1$、$f_2$ 仍是 $u,v$ 的函数，求二阶导时要再次用链式法则

### 答案 4 — 填空题（全微分）

$z = x^2y + y^3$

$$
\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} = 2xy, \quad \frac{\partial z}{\partial y} = x^2 + 3y^2
$$

在 $(1,2)$ 处：

$$
\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}\bigg|_{(1,2)} = 4, \quad \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}\bigg|_{(1,2)} = 13
$$

$$
\boxed{dz = 4dx + 13dy}
$$

### 答案 5 — 计算题（隐函数偏导数）

$x^2 + y^2 + z^2 - 4z = 0$

**方法：方程两边对 $x$ 求偏导**（$y$ 看常数，$z$ 为因变量）：

$$
2x + 0 + 2z\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} - 4\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} = 0
$$

$$
\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}(2z - 4) = -2x
$$

$$
\boxed{\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} = \frac{-2x}{2z - 4} = \frac{x}{2 - z}}
$$

### 答案 6 — 计算题（二元函数极值）

$f(x,y) = x^3 - y^3 + 3x^2 + 3y^2 - 9x$

**步骤 1：求驻点**

$$
f_x = 3x^2 + 6x - 9 = 0 \Rightarrow x = 1 \text{ 或 } x = -3
$$

$$
f_y = -3y^2 + 6y = 0 \Rightarrow y = 0 \text{ 或 } y = 2
$$

驻点：$(1,0)$、$(1,2)$、$(-3,0)$、$(-3,2)$

**步骤 2：判别**

$$
f_{xx} = 6x + 6, \quad f_{xy} = 0, \quad f_{yy} = -6y + 6
$$

| 驻点 | $A$ | $B$ | $C$ | $\Delta = AC - B^2$ | 结论 |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|------|
| $(1,0)$ | $12$ | $0$ | $6$ | $72 > 0$，$A > 0$ | **极小值** $f = -5$ |
| $(1,2)$ | $12$ | $0$ | $-6$ | $-72 < 0$ | 不是极值 |
| $(-3,0)$ | $-12$ | $0$ | $6$ | $-72 < 0$ | 不是极值 |
| $(-3,2)$ | $-12$ | $0$ | $-6$ | $72 > 0$，$A < 0$ | **极大值** $f = 31$ |

### 答案 7 — 计算题（条件极值）

$f(x,y) = xy$，条件 $x + y = 1$

**方法 1：拉格朗日乘数法**

$$
L = xy + \lambda(x + y - 1)
$$

$$
\begin{cases} L_x = y + \lambda = 0 \\ L_y = x + \lambda = 0 \\ L_\lambda = x + y - 1 = 0 \end{cases}
$$

由前两式：$y = x$

代入第三式：$2x = 1 \Rightarrow x = 1/2$，$y = 1/2$

$$
f(1/2, 1/2) = \boxed{1/4}
$$

**方法 2：代入法**

$y = 1 - x$，代入 $f = x(1-x) = x - x^2$

$\dfrac{df}{dx} = 1 - 2x = 0 \Rightarrow x = 1/2$，$y = 1/2$

$$
f_{\max} = \boxed{1/4}
$$

### 答案 8 — 计算题（极坐标二重积分）

$\displaystyle\iint_D (1 - \sqrt{x^2 + y^2}) \, dxdy$，$D$：$x^2 + y^2 \leqslant 1$，第一象限

**极坐标**：$x = r\cos\theta$，$y = r\sin\theta$，$dxdy = r \, drd\theta$

$$
0 \leqslant \theta \leqslant \frac{\pi}{2}, \quad 0 \leqslant r \leqslant 1
$$

$$
= \int_0^{\pi/2} d\theta \int_0^1 (1 - r) \cdot r \, dr = \int_0^{\pi/2} d\theta \int_0^1 (r - r^2) \, dr
$$

$$
= \int_0^{\pi/2} \left[\frac{r^2}{2} - \frac{r^3}{3}\right]_0^1 d\theta = \int_0^{\pi/2} \frac{1}{6} d\theta = \boxed{\frac{\pi}{12}}
$$

### 答案 9 — 计算题（极坐标高斯型）

$\displaystyle\iint_D e^{-(x^2+y^2)} \, dxdy$，$D$：$x^2 + y^2 \leqslant 4$

**极坐标**：$0 \leqslant \theta \leqslant 2\pi$，$0 \leqslant r \leqslant 2$

$$
= \int_0^{2\pi} d\theta \int_0^2 e^{-r^2} \cdot r \, dr = 2\pi \cdot \left[-\frac{1}{2}e^{-r^2}\right]_0^2
$$

$$
= 2\pi \cdot \frac{1}{2}(1 - e^{-4}) = \boxed{\pi(1 - e^{-4})}
$$

### 答案 10 — 计算题（交换积分次序）

$$
\int_0^1 dy \int_y^1 e^{x^2} \, dx
$$

$e^{x^2}$ 对 $x$ 积不出来 → 交换次序

**原区域**：$0 \leqslant y \leqslant 1$，$y \leqslant x \leqslant 1$

**交换后**：$0 \leqslant x \leqslant 1$，$0 \leqslant y \leqslant x$

$$
= \int_0^1 dx \int_0^x e^{x^2} \, dy = \int_0^1 e^{x^2} \cdot x \, dx
$$

令 $u = x^2$，$du = 2x \, dx$：

$$
= \frac{1}{2}\int_0^1 e^u \, du = \frac{1}{2}(e - 1) = \boxed{\frac{e - 1}{2}}
$$

### 答案 11 — 计算题（投影法）

$\displaystyle\iiint_\Omega z \, dV$，$\Omega$：$z = 0$、$z = 1$、$x^2 + y^2 = 1$

**投影法（先一后二）**：$D_{xy}$：$x^2 + y^2 \leqslant 1$

$$
= \iint_{D_{xy}} \left[\int_0^1 z \, dz\right] dxdy = \iint_{D_{xy}} \frac{1}{2} dxdy = \frac{1}{2} \cdot \pi \cdot 1^2 = \boxed{\frac{\pi}{2}}
$$

---

### 答案 12 — 计算题（截割法）

$\displaystyle\iiint_\Omega z^2 \, dV$，$\Omega$：$x^2 + y^2 + z^2 \leqslant 1$

**截割法（先二后一）**：截面 $D_z$：$x^2 + y^2 \leqslant 1 - z^2$，面积 $S(z) = \pi(1 - z^2)$

$$
= \int_{-1}^1 z^2 \cdot \pi(1 - z^2) \, dz = \pi\int_{-1}^1 (z^2 - z^4) \, dz
$$

$$
= \pi \cdot 2\int_0^1 (z^2 - z^4) \, dz = 2\pi\left[\frac{z^3}{3} - \frac{z^5}{5}\right]_0^1 = 2\pi \cdot \frac{2}{15} = \boxed{\frac{4\pi}{15}}
$$

### 答案 13 — 计算题（柱面坐标）

$\displaystyle\iiint_\Omega (x^2 + y^2) \, dV$，$\Omega$：$z = 0$、$z = 4 - x^2 - y^2$

**柱面坐标**：$x^2 + y^2 = r^2$，$z$ 从 $0$ 到 $4 - r^2$，$r$ 从 $0$ 到 $2$

$$
= \int_0^{2\pi} d\theta \int_0^2 r^2 \cdot r \, dr \int_0^{4-r^2} dz = 2\pi \int_0^2 r^3(4 - r^2) \, dr
$$

$$
= 2\pi \int_0^2 (4r^3 - r^5) \, dr = 2\pi\left[r^4 - \frac{r^6}{6}\right]_0^2 = 2\pi\left(16 - \frac{64}{6}\right) = 2\pi \cdot \frac{32}{6} = \boxed{\frac{32\pi}{3}}
$$

### 答案 14 — 计算题（球坐标）

$\displaystyle\iiint_\Omega z \, dV$，$\Omega$：$x^2 + y^2 + z^2 \leqslant 4$，第一卦限

**球坐标**：$z = r\cos\varphi$，$0 \leqslant r \leqslant 2$，$0 \leqslant \varphi \leqslant \pi/2$，$0 \leqslant \theta \leqslant \pi/2$

$$
= \int_0^{\pi/2} d\theta \int_0^{\pi/2} \sin\varphi \, d\varphi \int_0^2 r\cos\varphi \cdot r^2 \, dr
$$

$$
= \frac{\pi}{2} \cdot \int_0^{\pi/2} \cos\varphi\sin\varphi \, d\varphi \cdot \int_0^2 r^3 \, dr
$$

$$
= \frac{\pi}{2} \cdot \frac{1}{2} \cdot \left[\frac{r^4}{4}\right]_0^2 = \frac{\pi}{2} \cdot \frac{1}{2} \cdot 4 = \boxed{\pi}
$$
